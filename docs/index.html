Simulated groceries,
in your terminal.

SuperMarketCLI is a terminal supermarket I built to learn the basics of Java.

$curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DJTheron/SuperMarketCLI/main/install.sh | sh

Flags

-l, --list

Browse available products

Lists all available products -> short-product-name, name, price

-p, --product

Look up specifics

Returns all product information

-c, --cart Actions[add, remove, view, clear] short-product-name quantity

Put stuff in your cart

add, remove, view and clear items in your cart

-c, --cart ch/checkout

Checkout with fake money (more satisfaction to be added at some point in time very far from here)

Purchases and clears cart.
