open source · terminal-first

Grocery shopping,
from your terminal.

SuperMarketCLI is a command-line grocery cart — browse products, build a cart, check out. No app, no tabs, just flags.

$curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DJTheron/SuperMarketCLI/main/install.sh | sh

Commands

-l, --list

Browse products

List every product available, with shorthand name and price.

-p, --product

Look up a product

Get details on a single item by its shorthand name.

-c, --cart

Manage your cart

add, remove, clear, or view — build your cart one flag at a time.

-ch, --checkout

Check out

Purchase everything in your cart and clear it for next time.
